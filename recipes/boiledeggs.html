Boiled protein-rich eggs

A fast and efficient recipe for preparing boiled protein-rich, nutrient-dense eggs.

Ingredients:

  • Two or more eggs
  • Enough water to cover the eggs in a pot
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Put the eggs in a pot and cover them with cold water.
  2. Turn on the stove and warm the water until it boils.
  3. Boil for 5 to 7 minutes.
  4. Pour out the hot water and add cold water over the eggs.
  5. Pour out the cold water.
  6. Break the egg shells and add a pinch of salt to the eggs.
