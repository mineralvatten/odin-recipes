A delicious, homemade pizza

A simple and fast recipe for preparing a tasty tuna pizza for you and your company to enjoy. Bon appetit!

Ingredients:

  • 2 dls of water
  • 5 dls of flour
  • 1 spoonful of baking powder
  • 1-2 spoonfuls of salt
  • 0.5 dls of melted butter or sunflower oil
  • 2-3 cans of tuna (200+grams each)
  • 2-300 grams of grated cheese of your choice
  • tomato sauce
  • mold cheese
  • pineapple chunks

Steps:

  1. Mix the water, flour, salt, oil/butter and baking powder in a bowl.
  2. Turn your oven to 200 degrees Celsius.
  3. Spread the pizza dough in the bowl over a baking paper on a baking tray.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce over the pizza dough evenly.
  5. Add the cheese evenly over the pizza dough.
  6. Add the tuna evenly.
  7. Add the mold cheese and the pineapple chunks.
  8. Insert the baking tray in the heated up oven
  9. Remove the baking tray from the oven after 20-25 minutes
  10. Let the pizza cool down before you dive in. Bon appetit!
