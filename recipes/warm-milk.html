Warm milk

Nutritious warm milk

A simple recipe for warm milk.

Ingredients:

  • Milk

Steps:

  1. Pour the milk in a cup. Don't use a cup with materials that are dangerous in a microwave oven.
  2. Insert the milk-filled cup in a microwave oven and turn on the microwave oven for 40-60 seconds. Consult your microwave's manual or personal experience for the power output to use here.
  3. Once the milk is heated up, cautiously remove the cup from the microwave oven. Be wary of the hot contents.
